Colgate Athletics Celebrates Rise in Raider Academic Honor Roll
Colgate Athletics hosted the annual Raider Acadmeic Honor Roll Luncheon in a new location Tuesday: Cotterell Court. The number of student-athletes landing on this elite list now outnumbers the space provided by the Colgate University Hall of Presidents.
To be eligible for the Raiders Academic Honor Roll, members had to achieve a GPA of at least 3.25. The 2017 class of honorees comprises 491 student-athletes, cheerleaders and student trainers, including 42 who achieved perfect 4.0 grade-point averages in at least one semester last year.
